Civic Witness Charter

A simple, human-centered field protocol for witnessing harm without escalating it — designed for clarity, safety, and continuity.

Three roles

These can be held by individuals or groups in moments of civic distress, conflict, or harm:

  • The Witness — observes, stabilizes presence, avoids escalation
  • The Archivist / Journalist — records with evidentiary integrity
  • The Guardian — watches safety, distance, exits, de-escalation conditions

Design intent

This project is intentionally lightweight, non-ideological, and adaptable. The goal is accuracy, safety, and continuity — not commentary, performance, or punishment.
